class Food

Attributes
weight (integer)
flavor (string)
aroma (string)
delicious (boolean)
shape (string)

Methods
cut
(By cutting the food, the weight and shape attributes are modified)

taste
(By tasting the food, the flavor and delicious attributes are modified)

spoil
(By letting the food spoil, the flavor, aroma, and delicious attributes are modified)

cook
(By cooking the food, all of the above attributes are modified)
