Methods

(By adding liquid to the glass, we modify the current_volume, total_weight, liquid_weight, empty, and color attributes)

(By drinking liquid from the glass, we modify the total_weight, current_volume, liquid_weight, and empty attributes)

(By breaking the glass, we modify the max_volume, color, total_weight, liquid_weight, current_volume, and empty attributes)
